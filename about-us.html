Since 2010, Toddlers Academy nursery and kindergarten have been a trusted choice in Sofia, offering a warm, engaging, and professional bilingual preschool environment. We provide instruction in both English and Bulgarian across a variety of subjects, supporting your child's early learning journey with care and expertise.

Committed to delivering the best modern kindergarten experience, we pride ourselves on offering a high-quality, international, and bilingual education in a safe, nurturing, and state-of-the-art setting with excellent amenities.

For Bulgarian families, Toddlers Academy is fully approved by the Ministry of Education and meets all health and safety standards required by law.

We understand that choosing the right kindergarten is an important decision. That's why we tailor our curriculum and environment to meet each child's unique needs and the expectations of every family we serve.

Our dynamic bilingual program encourages cognitive, social, emotional, and physical growth through structured and free play. We nurture creativity, discovery, problem-solving, independence, self-esteem, and kindness. Our thoughtfully designed curriculum sparks curiosity and a love of learning with fun, imaginative activities that support early brain development and collaborative play.

Our Mission

At Toddlers Academy nursery and kindergarten, we aim to provide young children with a stimulating, exciting, and safe environment, where they can discover, communicate and learn to express verbally and emotionally themselves at many levels. Play is essential part of this learning process and is used to extend their boundaries, through which children can begin to understand both themselves and the world around them – progressively moving towards becoming one with it. Through play and activities, music and song, story and poetry, singing and dancing, experience and observation, interaction, and conversation, we lay the foundations on which the future personality of the child will be built. We also ensure that while moving along this path with the child, the parents, the family, and the home environment is fully integrated into the process by consistently meeting the parents, informing them of our work with their child and encouraging them to reinforce it at home as well.

Our Philosophy

These are the things we will learn as these are the things which will lay the foundations of a balanced and wholesome personality well-adjusted to the needs of the communities in which we live:

Share everything.
Play fair.
Do not hit people.
Put things back where you found them.
Clean up your own mess.
Do not take things that are not yours.
When you go out in the world, watch out for traffic, hold hands and stick together.
