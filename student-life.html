Student life

8:00 - 9:00

Arrival & Circle time

On arrival, children join their teachers' group and head to their age-appropriate rooms. The day starts with "Circle time," where kids engage in songs, stories, games, and activities that boost verbal, listening, and social skills. This special time encourages communication, attention, new learning, and lots of fun through movement, music, and play.

Each day, a varied and healthy breakfast is provided. The breakfast is provided by a reputable caterer, approved by the Ministry of Health.

9:00 - 9:30

Breakfast

Each day, a varied and healthy breakfast is provided. The breakfast is provided by a reputable caterer, approved by the Ministry of Health.

9:30 - 11:30

Bulgarian & English classes

Depending on their group, children attend either Bulgarian or English classes according to their specific program. Daily teaching in Bulgarian follows the official curriculum approved by the Bulgarian Ministry of Education, ensuring compliance with national standards.

Meanwhile, English classes follow the Oxford International Program, designed to support non-native English learners with immersive language development alongside other key skills. This dual approach allows children to benefit from both educational systems, fostering strong language skills, cognitive growth, and social-emotional development in a supportive, bilingual environment.

10:00 - 10:15

Fruit time

A daily routine that provides a healthy energy boost by serving fresh fruits to the children.

11:30 - 12:00

Additional activities

Children enjoy additional activities such as yoga, dance, sports, or art classes, depending on the day. Alternatively, they spend time outside engaging in free play.

12:15 - 13:00

Lunch

The main meal of the day, lunch is a time to relax and to unwind from all the activities of the morning session. A varied and healthy lunch is provided. The lunch is provided by a reputable caterer, approved by the Ministry of Health.

13:00 - 15:00

Siesta

Siesta time for all children in kindergarten. It aims to calm the nervous system of children and to restore the energy levels used during the early part of the day.

15:00 - 15:30

Tea time

Tea time and the late afternoon snack is part of the overall daily routine and aims to provide an energy boost for children for the remainder of the day.

15:30 - 17:00

Academic lessons

We hold more academic lessons in either English or Bulgarian. These afternoon sessions focus on games and practical activities designed to sustain children's interest and attention, helping to develop their focus and concentration skills.

17:00 - 18:30

Play time

Children spend time playing outdoors in the secure garden area, enjoying fresh air and physical activity while being closely supervised by their teachers.

18:30

Dispatch

Children are picked up at the end of the day and depart in a calm, familiar rhythm after play and guided activities.
