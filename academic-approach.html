Academic Approach

Our academic approach is thoughtfully put together using the latest research to make bilingual learning in Bulgarian and English both effective and enjoyable. It blends proven teaching methods with fresh ideas to support academic growth, cultural understanding, and overall development. By combining new techniques with traditional values, we help children make steady progress while meeting global learning standards.

Key Features

Active Learning

Children take an active role in their learning, encouraging curiosity, confidence, and engagement.

Play-Based Learning

Play is central to the process where real-life experiences build practical learning and joy.

Creativity and Innovation

We cultivate creativity through inquiry and expression while embracing innovative learning tools and ideas.

Teacher Support and Inclusivity

Teaching is inclusive and responsive, ensuring each child feels seen and supported in a welcoming environment.

Play-Based Methods

Research-backed, playful methods keep every child engaged and motivated to learn.

This approach not only meets educational standards but also respects and builds on each child’s cultural and language background, helping them feel confident and excited about learning. We support early bilingual skills and encourage an appreciation for different cultures, setting the stage for happy, curious learners ready to explore the world around them.

Oxford International Program

The Oxford International Program, developed by Oxford University Press, offers a world-class curriculum for children aged 2 to 7 who are non-native English speakers. It combines English language learning with key developmental areas, fostering communication, creativity, and critical thinking while respecting children’s native languages.

By fostering academic, social, and emotional growth, the Oxford International Program empowers children to thrive in an international and multicultural environment.

01

Language and Literacy Development

Develops oral and written skills through storytelling, early writing, vocabulary, and dialogue to build strong communication foundations.

02

Phonics Learning

Builds counting, number recognition, shape identification, spatial and logical thinking through grouping, sequencing, and problem-solving activities.

03

Mathematical Thinking

Combines natural sciences and social studies to foster understanding of the environment, health, safety, professions, civic responsibility, teamwork, emotional intelligence, and independence.

04

World Exploration

Stimulates creativity and aesthetic appreciation through painting, drawing, and sculpture, encouraging artistic expression and emotional engagement.

05

Creative Expression

Enhances fine motor skills and technical knowledge through hands-on activities like model building, woodworking, and basic technology use.

06

Social and Emotional Skills

Supports physical growth, coordination, and healthy habits via structured activities and games.

  • Why Choose Oxford International?
  • Globally Trusted: Developed by Oxford University Press, a leader in education.
  • Holistic Development: Combines language learning with academic, social, and emotional growth.
  • Future-Ready: Prepares children for primary education and beyond in an international environment.

Parent Involvement
